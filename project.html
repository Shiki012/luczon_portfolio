CBMIS

City-wide Barangay Management Information System

      This is a website mainly focused on helping the Manila Barangay Bureau. It is intended to process a wide variety of information pertaining to barangay official, barangay certificates or permits, Barangay blotter or complaints, as well as the required reports from the barangay that are required to be submitted to City Manila Barangay Bureau.
